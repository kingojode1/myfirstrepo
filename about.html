WHO ARE WE?

Newbrain.com is a football prediction and analysis company that is concerned about what you get when you pay money for football predictions. We have a team of experts whose job is to primarily take a deep dive into most of the major football leagues around the world.

For some time now, football has become an enormous business that now carry a lot of weight. It is no longer what it used to be in the past when it was basically for fun; it has become much bigger than that, little wonder some football prediction companies, including newbrain.com, have decided to take it upon themselves to critically analyse the matches that always come thick and thin in order to give you an excellent result.

WHY ARE WE DOING THIS?

Newbrain.com disvered some couple of years ago that as much as people are watching the game of football, they would also want to make some dollars off it. As soon as some very wealthy individuals started putting money into football by buying some of the clubs in Europe and beyond, we sensed that it would have some consequencies which are usually determined from behind the scene. And since newbrain a lot of informants in the major leagues, have decided to share this information with you so that you can make money while we do what also helps us to make money.
